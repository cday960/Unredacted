Let the truth be known

A better way to find declassified documents

Do you want to know more about a topic in American history? Is searching through the National Archives a headache?

Then you're in the right place.

Unredacted allows you to quickly find declassified documents, read summaries on articles, and find the information you're looking for faster. Unredacted was started as a capstone project at the University of Missouri, and is currently maintained by four senior computer science students.
